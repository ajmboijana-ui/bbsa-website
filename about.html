About BBSA

We are Vanderbilt's home for Black business students — a community built on ambition, access, and the belief that representation in business starts here.

Who We Are

Mission & Vision

Mission

What We Stand For

Vanderbilt's Black Business Student Association (BBSA) is deeply committed to amplifying access to meaningful career opportunities in the business sector for Black students. Through focused initiatives, educational workshops, and impactful partnerships with industry leaders, we connect our members with the vital resources and networks they need to succeed.

Vision

Where We're Going

To be the premier pipeline for Black business talent at Vanderbilt University and beyond — cultivating the next generation of leaders in finance, consulting, entrepreneurship, and technology who carry their community with them as they rise.

Our Work

What We Do

BBSA operates across four core pillars, each designed to move members closer to their professional goals.

Professional Development

Resume reviews, mock interviews, case prep, and skill workshops that give members a competitive edge when recruiting season arrives.

Corporate Partnerships

Direct relationships with top firms in finance, consulting, and technology — giving members access to internship pipelines and full-time opportunities.

Community Building

A space of belonging where Black business students find mentors, peers, and a network that lasts long after graduation.

Career Exploration

Industry panels, career treks, and alumni connections that help members discover and pursue the right path in business — before the pressure of recruiting hits.
