Questions

Frequently Asked

Who can join BBSA? +
BBSA is open to all currently enrolled Vanderbilt University students, regardless of major, school, year, or background. You do not need to be a business major. If you have an interest in business and want to build your career alongside a community of driven peers, you belong here.
Is there a membership fee? +
Membership details including any dues are communicated at the beginning of each semester during our first General Body Meeting. Reach out to vanderbiltbbsa@gmail.com for the most current information.
When does recruiting start? +
Finance and consulting recruiting typically begins in the fall semester, often earlier than students expect. BBSA's GBMs and workshops are designed to keep you ahead of deadlines. Join early — ideally your first or second year — to maximize your time building relationships with partner firms.
How do I apply for a leadership position? +
Executive board elections are held each spring. All active members in good standing are eligible to run. Announcements go out via email and Instagram. Email vanderbiltbbsa@gmail.com if you'd like to learn more about what each role involves.
