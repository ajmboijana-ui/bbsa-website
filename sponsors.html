Why Partner

The Case for Partnership

Partnering with BBSA means direct access to Vanderbilt's top Black business talent. We offer recruitment pipelines, brand visibility, and the opportunity to invest in the next generation of diverse business leaders. Our members are recruited by the world's leading firms — and they remember the companies that showed up for them early.

Talent Pipeline

Direct access to 100+ high-achieving Black business students across all Vanderbilt programs.

Brand Visibility

Exposure across BBSA's GBMs, events, newsletter, and social media reaching students and alumni.

Community Impact

Demonstrate a genuine commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion that goes beyond messaging.

How to Donate

Contributions to BBSA directly fund Career Trek travel, workshop programming, event costs, and member resources. All donations are processed through Vanderbilt University.

1 Online Donation
Visit give.vanderbilt.edu and search for "Black Business Student Association" in the fund search bar. Contributions of any amount are welcome and fully processed through Vanderbilt's secure giving portal.
2 Check
Make checks payable to Vanderbilt University.
In the memo line, write: "Black Business Student Association, Task #382816"
