Our Mission

Built for Black Business Excellence

Vanderbilt's Black Business Student Association is deeply committed to amplifying access to meaningful career opportunities in the business sector for Black students. Through focused initiatives, educational workshops, and impactful partnerships with industry leaders, we connect our members with the vital resources and networks they need to succeed.
