Events & Programs

Every BBSA program is built with one goal: to close the gap between where our members are and where they want to be professionally.

[General Body Meeting Photo]
Program 01

General Body Meetings

Our biweekly gatherings where members connect, hear from corporate partners, and stay updated on recruitment and career opportunities. GBMs are the heartbeat of BBSA — a reliable touchpoint for community, information, and momentum throughout the semester.

Each meeting features updates from the executive board, spotlights on upcoming recruiting timelines, and often includes a guest speaker or panel from our partner companies.

Biweekly during the academic semester
Vanderbilt University Campus — location announced via email
Join to Attend
[Career Trek 2026 Photo]
Program 02

Career Trek

Our flagship annual trip where BBSA members visit top companies in a major city for office tours, panels with executives and Black professionals, and direct networking. Career Trek is a transformative experience that turns awareness of a career path into a concrete, achievable goal.

Past destinations include Chicago, with visits to McKinsey, BCG, Deloitte, Google, BMO, Northern Trust, and Kearney. Members leave with business cards, referrals, and a real sense of what these careers look like from the inside.

Annual — typically held in the spring semester
Major U.S. financial & consulting hub — city TBA each year
Open to all active BBSA members — limited spots available

Program 03

Workshops & Seminars

Skill-building sessions covering resume writing, interview preparation, case interviews, and industry-specific knowledge — led by professionals, recruiters, and alumni who know what top firms are actually looking for.

Our workshops are designed to meet members where they are, from first-years exploring business to seniors finalizing offers. Topics are tailored to what recruiters and industry insiders tell us matters most.

Topics Include
Resume Building Behavioral Interviews Case Interview Prep Finance Fundamentals Networking Strategy Personal Branding
Throughout the academic year — announced via email and Instagram
Join to Attend
[Networking Dinner Photo]
Program 04

Networking Dinners

Intimate dinners connecting BBSA members with industry leaders, Vanderbilt alumni, and professionals across finance, consulting, and technology. These curated evenings are intentionally small — so every member gets real face time, not just a business card.

Past dinners have featured professionals from investment banking, private equity, management consulting, and tech. Attendees consistently report these events as their most impactful BBSA experience.

Once or twice per semester — RSVP required
Seats are limited — priority given to active members
